Our Story

Dream Makers is a registered independent, non-governmental and non-profitable social organization established by a group of like-minded people with experiences in different sectors to promote health, Education and Empowerment and Climate Change in Kenya. We aspire to improve the living and health conditions of the poor and marginalized groups by adopting strategies through community initiative, participation and sensitization. Dream Makers comprises of diverse and dynamic group of competent people from different background. It has ten executive members. Each member has specific qualities that will help to sustain and run the organization. From health to management, nursing to administration, social sciences and information technology
